<p><p>The flexible electrical conduits market is divided into metal and non-metal categories. Metal conduits, typically made from materials like aluminum or steel, offer enhanced durability, resistance to physical damage, and are suitable for outdoor applications. Non-metal conduits, often constructed from PVC or similar polymers, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for indoor installations where moisture and chemical exposure are concerns. Both types cater to diverse electrical protection needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
