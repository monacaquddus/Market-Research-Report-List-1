Fishing trawlers utilize large nets dragged along the seafloor to catch a variety of fish and seafood. Longliners deploy baited hooks on long lines, targeting specific species and minimizing bycatch. Dredgers use heavy equipment to scrape the ocean floor, primarily targeting shellfish like clams and oysters. Each market type specializes in different fishing methods, catering to diverse seafood demands and sustainability practices.</p></p>
