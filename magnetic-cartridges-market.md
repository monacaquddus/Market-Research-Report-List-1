<p><strong>The Magnetic Cartridges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Magnet (MM)</li><li>Moving Coil (MC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic cartridges market is primarily divided into two main types: Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC). Moving Magnet cartridges utilize a magnet attached to the stylus, generating an electrical signal when it moves over the record grooves, offering a warmer sound and easier replacement. In contrast, Moving Coil cartridges have a lightweight coil attached to the stylus that moves within a fixed magnetic field, providing higher fidelity and detail, but often at a higher cost and with more complex replacement needs.</p></p>
