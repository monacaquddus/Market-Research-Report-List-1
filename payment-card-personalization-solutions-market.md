<p><p>Payment Card Personalization Solutions encompass various technologies for producing customized payment cards. Dye-sublimation uses heat to transfer dye onto cards, yielding vibrant, durable images ideal for high-volume production. Retransfer printing involves printing on a film that is then applied to the card, offering superior image quality and the ability to cover the entire card surface. Inkjet technology allows for direct printing onto cards, suitable for lower-volume, on-demand personalization with flexibility in design and quick turnaround times.</p></p>
