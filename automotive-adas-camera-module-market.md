<p><p>The Automotive ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) camera module market primarily includes CCD (Charged Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for sensing applications in low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS cameras are gaining popularity due to their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and compact size, enabling real-time image processing. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and facilitating features like lane departure warnings and parking assistance.</p></p>
