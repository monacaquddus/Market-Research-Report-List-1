<p><p>Autostereoscopic outdoor displays utilize various technologies to create 3D visual experiences without the need for glasses. Parallax barrier displays use a layered screen that directs different images to each eye, creating depth perception. Integral photography captures light fields, allowing views from multiple angles, producing a more immersive experience. Lenticular arrays manipulate light with cylindrical lenses to create the illusion of depth. Other markets may include hybrid systems or emerging technologies aimed at enhancing visual presentation in outdoor environments.</p></p>
