<p><p>The valve spool market encompasses three primary types: manual, mechanical, and electromagnetic valve spools. Manual valve spools allow operators to control flow manually, providing simplicity and reliability in various applications. Mechanical valve spools utilize mechanical linkages to automate operation, enhancing efficiency in systems requiring precision. Electromagnetic valve spools leverage electromagnetic forces for responsive control, ideal for automation and advanced applications.
