<p><p>Guidance barriers are essential for crowd management and are primarily categorized into two types: tape guidance barriers and rope guidance barriers. Tape guidance barriers are flexible and lightweight, often used in indoor settings to create temporary queues or designated walking paths. In contrast, rope guidance barriers exude a more formal appearance, typically featuring stanchions and velvet ropes, making them suitable for upscale events or locations. Both types facilitate organized movement and enhance crowd control in various environments, from events to retail spaces.</p></p>
