<p><p>The dishwashing detergent tablet market can be segmented into two main types: saponification and non-saponification. Saponification-based tablets utilize fats and oils that undergo a chemical reaction with alkalis to produce soap. These tend to be biodegradable and eco-friendly. In contrast, non-saponification tablets rely on synthetic surfactants and enzymes for cleaning, providing effective stain removal and grease cutting properties. This differentiation caters to various consumer preferences for sustainability versus effectiveness in tough cleaning tasks.</p></p>
