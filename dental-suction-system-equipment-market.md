<p><strong>The Dental Suction System Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental suction system equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: wet and dry systems. Wet suction systems utilize water to create a vacuum and are effective for continuous suctioning during dental procedures, often requiring more maintenance. In contrast, dry suction systems rely on air pressure to generate vacuum, offering a cleaner, more energy-efficient solution with lower water consumption. Both types are essential in dental practices for maintaining hygiene and facilitating various procedures, but they cater to different operational needs.</p></p>
