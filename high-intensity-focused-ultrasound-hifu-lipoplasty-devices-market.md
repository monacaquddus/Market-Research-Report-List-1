<p><p>High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) lipoplasty devices utilize focused ultrasonic waves to target and destroy fat cells non-invasively. The market is segmented into two primary types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided devices. Ultrasound-guided devices use real-time imaging to precisely direct ultrasound energy to the targeted area, ensuring accuracy in fat removal. In contrast, MR-guided devices utilize magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced visualization, allowing for improved targeting and treatment planning. Both approaches aim to provide effective fat reduction with minimal recovery time.</p></p>
