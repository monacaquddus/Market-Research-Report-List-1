<p><p>Synthetic proppants are durable materials used in hydraulic fracturing to prop open fractures in geological formations, enhancing the flow of oil and gas. Unlike traditional proppants like sand, synthetic proppants, which are typically made from materials such as resin-coated particles or ceramic, offer superior strength, uniformity, and lower degradation rates.
