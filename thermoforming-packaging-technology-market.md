<p><p>Thermoforming packaging technology encompasses three primary market types: vacuum packaging, pressure packaging, and mechanical packaging. Vacuum packaging involves removing air from the packaging to enhance shelf life and protect products from contamination. Pressure packaging utilizes increased pressure to maintain product integrity and extend freshness, often used for fragile items. Mechanical packaging relies on the physical manipulation of materials to shape and seal them, providing versatility in design and functionality. Together, these methods offer tailored solutions for efficient and protective packaging.</p></p>
