<p><strong>The Interstitial Cystitis Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral Therapy</li><li>Intravesical Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interstitial cystitis drugs market is primarily segmented into oral therapy and intravesical therapy. Oral therapy involves systemic medications such as analgesics, anti-inflammatories, and other oral agents aimed at alleviating symptoms and improving bladder function. In contrast, intravesical therapy delivers medications directly into the bladder through catheterization, providing localized treatment and potentially reducing systemic side effects. Both approaches aim to manage discomfort and improve the quality of life for patients suffering from interstitial cystitis.</p></p>
