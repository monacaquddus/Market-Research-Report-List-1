<p><p>The semi-fluid lubricant market consists of three main types: mineral oil-based, semisynthetic oil-based, and others. Semisynthetic oil-based lubricants blend mineral oils with synthetic additives, enhancing performance and thermal stability. The "others" category includes bio-based and specialty lubricants, catering to specific applications and environmental concerns. Together, these types serve diverse sectors, including automotive, industrial, and machinery lubrication.</p></p>
