<p><p>The qPCR kits market can be categorized based on several methodologies. Affinity purification focuses on isolating specific nucleic acids using specific binding agents. Antibody labeling kits utilize antibodies tagged with fluorescent markers to enhance detection sensitivity. Block/neutralize/inhibit kits are designed to reduce non-specific amplification, improving assay accuracy. Cell culture kits facilitate the growth and maintenance of cells for sample analysis. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses various emerging technologies and specialized applications that do not fit neatly into the other segments.</p></p>
