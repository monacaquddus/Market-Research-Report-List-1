<p><p>The cloud seeding equipment market is primarily divided into two types: aerial and ground-based systems. Aerial cloud seeding equipment includes aircraft and drones equipped for dispersing seeding agents directly into the atmosphere, facilitating targeted precipitation enhancement. Ground-based cloud seeding equipment consists of stationary or mobile units that utilize generators to release seeding materials into updrafts, aiming to induce rainfall from clouds. Both types serve to augment water supply, especially in agricultural and drought-prone regions.</p></p>
