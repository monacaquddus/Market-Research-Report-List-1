Spot type sensors are designed to detect liquid presence at specific locations, offering targeted monitoring for potential leaks. In contrast, cable type sensors feature a continuous sensing cable that can cover larger areas, providing extensive coverage and immediate alerts along its length. These technologies are crucial for preventing damage in various industries by ensuring timely detection of leaks in critical environments.</p></p>
