<p><p>Calcium Phosphide fumigation involves using various formulations for pest control, primarily available in liquid and solid forms. Liquid calcium phosphide is typically mixed with water to create a fumigant that effectively targets pests in enclosed spaces. In contrast, solid calcium phosphide is used in granular form, emitting phosphine gas upon exposure to moisture, making it suitable for bulk grain storage or agricultural applications. Both types are crucial in managing pest infestations, ensuring product safety and quality during storage and transport.</p></p>
