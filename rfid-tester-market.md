<p><strong>The RFID Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>High Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>Ultra-High Frequency RFID Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tester market is categorized into three main types based on frequency ranges: Low Frequency (LF) RFID testers operate around 125-134 kHz, suitable for short-range applications like access control and animal tracking. High Frequency (HF) RFID testers work at 13.56 MHz, commonly used for smart cards and NFC applications. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID testers function between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, enabling longer read distances and faster data transfer, making them ideal for supply chain management and inventory tracking.</p></p>
