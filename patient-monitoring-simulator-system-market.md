Standard Vital Signs simulators provide predefined, essential physiological parameters like heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation, ensuring baseline training for healthcare providers. In contrast, Customizable Vital Signs simulators offer flexibility, allowing users to modify parameters according to specific scenarios or patient conditions. This adaptability enhances training realism and addresses varied clinical situations, catering to diverse educational needs in medical environments.</p></p>
