<p><p>The travel bus market comprises three main types: electric buses, fuel buses, and hybrid buses. Electric buses operate on battery power, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs but requiring charging infrastructure. Fuel buses, typically running on diesel or gasoline, provide high power and range but contribute to air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. Hybrid buses combine internal combustion engines with electric propulsion, balancing efficiency and reduced emissions, making them an effective transitional solution towards greener public transport.</p></p>
