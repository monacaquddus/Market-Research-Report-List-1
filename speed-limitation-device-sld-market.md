<p><p>Speed Limitation Devices (SLDs) are systems designed to restrict vehicle speed for safety and regulatory compliance. The market can be categorized into two main types: Fuel Type Speed Limiters and Electronic Pedal Interface Speed Limiters. Fuel Type Speed Limiters regulate engine power based on fuel consumption, automatically limiting speed to optimize efficiency while ensuring safety. In contrast, Electronic Pedal Interface Speed Limiters directly control the accelerator's functionality, providing a more precise method for managing vehicle speed and enhancing driver compliance.</p></p>
