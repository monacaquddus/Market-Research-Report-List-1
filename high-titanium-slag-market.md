<p><ul><li>Acid Soluble Slag</li><li>Chlorination Slag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high titanium slag market consists primarily of two types: acid soluble slag and chlorination slag. Acid soluble slag is processed to extract titanium dioxide through acid leaching, making it valuable for industries like pigments and titanium metal production. Chlorination slag, on the other hand, involves a process that converts the titanium into chlorides, which can be further refined. Both types serve specific applications in the metallurgy and chemical industries, contributing to the overall demand for titanium products.</p></p>
