<p><p>The electric handpieces market comprises two primary types: high-speed and low-speed electric handpieces. High-speed electric handpieces are designed for efficient cutting and shaping of dental materials, often featuring speeds exceeding 100,000 RPM, making them ideal for procedures such as cavity preparation. Conversely, low-speed electric handpieces operate at lower RPMs, typically between 20,000 to 40,000 RPM, and are used for polishing, finishing, and tooth preparation. Both types enhance precision and reduce operator fatigue, catering to various dental procedures and specialties.</p></p>
