<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nuclear Medicine Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Positron Emission Tomography (PET) Camera"</li><li>"Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) Camera"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear medicine camera market is primarily categorized into two types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) cameras and Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) cameras. PET cameras use radioactive tracers to produce detailed 3D images of metabolic processes, primarily in oncology and cardiology, while SPECT cameras capture images using gamma rays emitted from radiopharmaceuticals to assess blood flow and diagnose conditions like heart disease and neurological disorders. Both technologies play crucial roles in advanced medical imaging and diagnostics.</p></p>
