<p><p>The Rapid Prototyping Equipment Market encompasses various technologies, including Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA) which uses UV light to cure liquid resin into solid models, and Three Dimension Printing (3DP) that applies inkjet technology to create parts layer by layer. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) employs laser beams to fuse powdered materials, while Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) layers adhesive-coated sheets to build prototypes. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic filament to form objects. Other methods may include material jetting and binder jetting technologies.</p></p>
