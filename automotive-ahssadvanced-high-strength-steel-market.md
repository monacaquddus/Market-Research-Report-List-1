<p><p>The automotive AHSS market comprises several advanced high-strength steel types, each offering unique properties for vehicle manufacturing. Dual Phase Steel features a mix of soft and hard phases, providing excellent formability and strength. Martensitic Steel, known for its high strength and hardness, is ideal for safety-critical components. Boron Steel, alloyed with boron, enhances toughness and hardenability. TRIP Steel (Transformation Induced Plasticity) combines strength with ductility, making it suitable for complex shapes and improving overall vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
