<p><p>The parking sensors market primarily consists of electromagnetic and ultrasonic reversing sensors. Electromagnetic parking sensors utilize electromagnetic fields to detect obstacles and alert drivers, providing a seamless installation with minimal visual impact. In contrast, ultrasonic reversing sensors emit sound waves to measure the distance to objects, offering precise feedback through audio or visual alerts. Both types enhance vehicle safety and maneuverability, catering to different consumer preferences for technology and installation methods in various vehicle models.</p></p>
