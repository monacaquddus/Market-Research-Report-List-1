<p><p>Cylindrical photoelectric sensors are essential in automation, primarily categorized into two types: diffuse type sensors and diffusion type sensors. Diffuse type sensors detect objects by emitting light that reflects off the target back to the sensor, suited for short-range applications. Conversely, diffusion type sensors utilize a broader light beam, allowing them to detect larger areas or objects at varied distances. Both types are crucial for various industrial applications, ensuring efficient object detection and position sensing in automated processes.</p></p>
