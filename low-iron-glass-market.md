<p><strong>The Low Iron Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Glass</li><li>Rolled Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Iron Glass market primarily includes Float Glass and Rolled Glass types. Float Glass is produced by floating molten glass on tin, resulting in a clear, distortion-free surface, ideal for applications requiring high transparency, such as architecture and electronics. Rolled Glass, on the other hand, is made by rolling molten glass into sheets, providing a textured surface and enhancing aesthetic appeal. Both types emphasize low iron content, which increases light transmission and reduces green tint, making them desirable for various industries.</p></p>
