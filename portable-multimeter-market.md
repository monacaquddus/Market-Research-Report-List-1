<p><ul><li>Digital Multimeter</li><li>Analog Multimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable multimeter market is primarily divided into two types: digital multimeters (DMMs) and analog multimeters. Digital multimeters provide precise readings on a digital display, making them user-friendly and accurate for measuring voltage, current, and resistance. They often include advanced features like data logging and auto-ranging. In contrast, analog multimeters use a needle and scale to indicate measurements, offering a more traditional approach. While they can be less precise, they are valued for their simplicity and durability in various applications.</p></p>
