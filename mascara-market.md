<p><p>The mascara market comprises three primary types: regular, waterproof, and water-resistant. Regular mascara offers easy application and removal, ideal for daily use. Waterproof mascara is designed for long-lasting wear, resisting moisture, tears, and sweat, making it suitable for events or activities that may cause smudging. Water-resistant mascara sits between the two, providing some moisture resistance while allowing easier removal than waterproof formulas. Each type caters to different consumer needs based on lifestyle, climate, and occasion preferences.</p></p>
