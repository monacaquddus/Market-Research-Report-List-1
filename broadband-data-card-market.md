<p><strong>The Broadband Data Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GSM</li><li>CDMA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The broadband data card market consists of two primary types: GSM and CDMA. GSM data cards utilize Global System for Mobile Communications, leveraging SIM cards for connectivity, offering widespread global compatibility and seamless switching between networks. In contrast, CDMA data cards operate on Code Division Multiple Access technology, which does not require a SIM card, providing better security and efficiency in signal use. Both types cater to different user preferences, with GSM favored for mobility and CDMA for reliability in specific regions.</p></p>
