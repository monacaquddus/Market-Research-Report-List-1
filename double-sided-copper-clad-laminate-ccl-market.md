<p><p>Double sided Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) is a crucial material used in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards (PCBs), offering conductive properties on both sides of the substrate. It consists of a dielectric material, typically epoxy resin reinforced with fiberglass, that is coated with copper on both surfaces. This configuration allows for higher circuit density and improved electrical performance, making it essential in various electronic applications, including consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and industrial devices.</p><p>The Double sided Copper Clad Laminate CCL Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.90% during the forecast period.
