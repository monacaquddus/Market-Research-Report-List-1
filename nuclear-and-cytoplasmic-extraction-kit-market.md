<p><p>The Nuclear and Cytoplasmic Extraction Kit market is categorized into three main types: Density Gradient Centrifugation, Chemical Lysis, and Mechanical Lysis. Density Gradient Centrifugation efficiently separates organelles based on their density, providing high-purity extracts. Chemical Lysis involves the use of specific reagents to disrupt cell membranes, allowing selective extraction of nuclear and cytoplasmic components. Mechanical Lysis employs physical methods, such as homogenization or sonication, to break open cells and release their contents. Each method offers unique advantages for cellular component isolation.</p></p>
