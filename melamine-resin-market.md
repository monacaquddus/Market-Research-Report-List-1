<p><strong>The Melamine Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Methylated Melamine Resin</li><li>Non-methylated Melamine Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melamine resin market is segmented into two main types: methylated melamine resin and non-methylated melamine resin. Methylated melamine resin is modified with methyl groups, enhancing thermal stability and water resistance, making it ideal for coatings, adhesives, and laminates. In contrast, non-methylated melamine resin lacks these modifications, providing lower stability and water resistance but often being more cost-effective, suitable for lower-end applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
