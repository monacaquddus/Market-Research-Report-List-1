<p><strong>The Shotcrete-sprayed Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Mix Shotcrete-sprayed Concrete</li><li>Dry Mix Shotcrete-sprayed Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shotcrete-sprayed concrete market is primarily divided into two types: wet mix and dry mix. Wet mix shotcrete involves premixing water, cement, and aggregates, which are then pumped through a nozzle for application. This method offers better adhesion and reduced dust. In contrast, dry mix shotcrete consists of dry ingredients mixed and then combined with water at the nozzle during application. This technique is generally quicker and can be easier to transport, making it suitable for a variety of construction projects.</p></p>
