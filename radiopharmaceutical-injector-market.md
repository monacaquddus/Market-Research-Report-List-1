<p><p>The radiopharmaceutical injector market encompasses devices used to administer radioactive drugs for diagnostic imaging and therapy, primarily in positron emission tomography (PET) and single photon emission computed tomography (SPECT). PET radioactive drugs are utilized for detecting metabolic activity in tissues, providing insights into conditions such as cancer. Both applications are crucial for accurate disease diagnosis, treatment monitoring, and improving patient outcomes in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
