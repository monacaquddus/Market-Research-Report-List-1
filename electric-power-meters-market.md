<p><p>The electric power meters market is categorized into three main types: Active Power Meters, which measure real power consumption in watts; Reactive Power Meters, which gauge the power used in reactive loads, important for inductive and capacitive applications; and Apparent Power Meters, which quantify the total power flowing in a system, combining both active and reactive power. Each type serves distinct roles in energy management, helping utilities and consumers monitor and optimize electrical usage efficiently.</p></p>
