<p><p>Next-generation multi-purpose biosensors encompass various types, each tailored for specific applications. Nanomechanical biosensors utilize nanoscale technologies for highly sensitive detection. Optical biosensors leverage light properties for real-time biomolecule analysis. Electrochemical biosensors convert biochemical interactions into electrical signals, enabling rapid testing. Thermistor/calorimetric biosensors monitor temperature changes during reactions, providing insights into metabolic processes. Other biosensor types may include piezoelectric and magnetic biosensors, expanding application versatility across healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety sectors, enhancing detection capabilities and user convenience.</p></p>
