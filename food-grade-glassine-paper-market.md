<p><p>The food-grade glassine paper market is categorized into three types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent glassine paper offers clarity, allowing for visibility of contents, making it ideal for packaging items that need to be displayed. Opaque glassine paper blocks light completely, ensuring better protection against moisture and contamination, making it perfect for products requiring enhanced preservation.</p></p>
